PRACTICE WITH AI CHAT

JAZZ

Practice Low Stakes

Practice conversations in a low stakes environment with an AI partner that dynamically responds to your practice sessions and teaches you improvisational skills and principles through real-time dialogue.

How it works:

  1. Set Goal (the conversation scenario) you want to complete successfully (e.g. job interview, first date, networking event)
  2. Learn improv skills (generic topics to learn principles)
  3. Choose improv exercise (e.g., yes-and, Questions Only, etc.)
  4. Session Settings: Voice (male or female), difficulty level, duration (minutes)
  5. Chat with the AI while it introduces unexpected conversational turns and challenges
  6. Receive feedback after sessions
  7. After several sessions, Jazz begins to focus on your scenario
REAL APPLICATION

ConvoQuest

Low Stakes Challenges

After the user learns new improv skills, the user will continue gaining confidence with real-world challenges that gamify building confidence through small, daily conversational missions. Real application, low stakes.

How it works:

  1. Unlock ConvoQuest after several sessions with Jazz
  2. Set your location and difficulty level
  3. Receive a daily "quest" like "Ask a barista a non-coffee related question" or "Give a stranger a genuine compliment."
  4. Go do the quest!
  5. Mark the quest as complete and briefly journal about how it felt
  6. Level up, unlock achievements, and see your confidence score grow over time
CONNECTION TO OTHERS

ImprovCircle

Real Practice with Community

Social community environment that allows you to connect with other users to practice improvisational skills and challenging conversations one-on-one or in group sessions. The user will continue gaining confidence and skills while building a network of friends and mentors who can support them with their original goal.

How it works:

  1. Unlock ImprovCircle after 8 successful quests
  2. Select your level and join a group
  3. Attend group sessions, guided by an improv expert (aka group improv classes remotely)
  4. Option to choose sessions for more specific scenarios (i.e., job interviews, first date)
